Disc Golf is a social activity!

Disc Golf is a very fun and welcoming community, especially in Maine. As a matter of fact, according to this survey the state of Maine
has the highest disc golfers per capita of anywhere in the world!

In order to show this, below is a video taken 2 years ago at a tournament I played at called "The McBeast Challenge", which is basically a couple of
professional players that tour around the country and hold these challenges.

This particular one, which was held at Pleasant Hill DGC in Scarborough Maine, was (and still is) the largest tournament of this type ever, with over 200 players!
